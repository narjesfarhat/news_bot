📰 3. 2026 Masters Tournament Begins at Augusta National
The 2026 Masters Tournament has officially begun at Augusta National Golf Club, marking the start of the year’s first major championship in golf. Defending champion Rory McIlroy returns after his historic 2025 victory, which secured him a career Grand Slam.
Top-ranked players, including Scottie Scheffler and Bryson DeChambeau, are among the leading contenders competing for the coveted green jacket. However, five-time champion Tiger Woods is notably absent due to injuries sustained in a recent accident.
The tournament runs from April 9 to April 12, with global audiences tuning in via television and live streaming platforms. Fans are closely watching early-round performances as players compete under intense pressure on one of golf’s most iconic courses.
Experts predict a highly competitive tournament, with several top players in strong form heading into the event.
