📰 4. World Snooker Championship Qualifiers Underway in Sheffield
The qualifying rounds for the 2026 World Snooker Championship are currently taking place in Sheffield, where 128 players are competing for 16 spots in the main tournament at the Crucible Theatre.
The qualifiers are especially critical for players outside the top 16 world rankings, as they must secure their place through multiple elimination rounds. Matches are played in a best-of-19 frames format, testing both skill and endurance.
Several notable players, including former champions, have already been eliminated early in the competition. Meanwhile, others aim to replicate past successes—such as Zhao Xintong, who famously won the championship after qualifying in a previous year.
The qualifying stages will continue over several days, with matches scheduled across morning, afternoon, and evening sessions.
